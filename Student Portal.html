BISD Students to Keep their Chromebooks Over the Summer

To encourage ongoing educational opportunities and alleviate distribution logistics, all BISD students will continue to have access to their Chromebooks by taking them home during the summer months. This opportunity was successfully piloted last summer by Eagle Harbor High School.

To assist you, the following are tips for summer Chromebook use:

Care

  • Keep Chromebooks away from pets or young children.
  • Avoid heat and direct sunlight.
  • Avoid liquids – ­Beware of wet swimsuits and towels.
  • Keep Chromebooks plugged into a surge protector when they are not in use.

Repairs/Damages

If you are experiencing software or hardware issues with your chromebook, please fill out this form and a technician will be in contact with you.

NOTE: While Chromebooks will be repaired as quickly as possible, replacements are not available over the summer.

Opt Out

If you choose to not to keep the device over the summer, you must return the chromebook, power cord, and bag by the last day of school. Any chromebooks left by 8th graders will be shipped to BHS for distribution in the Fall.

Insurance

If you purchased optional insurance for the chromebook, your policy is effective until August 31st.

Leaving the District

Please return your Chromebook, power cord, and bag to your school office at the end of the school year. If the device is not returned, you are responsible for the full replacement cost of the device: $350.

NOTE: All past tech tips can be found by going to "Additional Resources" and selecting the"Tech Support" page on this student portal.
